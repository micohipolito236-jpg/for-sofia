Why I Choose You

Sofia Mae, my feelings for you grew slowly, not from impulse but from admiration. I respect your strength, your dedication as a teacher, and the way you carry yourself with grace.

I admire your mind — the way you think, the way you guide others, and the way you stay grounded. It makes me want to become better, not just for you, but for myself.

I don’t offer temporary affection. I offer consistency. I offer patience. I offer effort.

If you allow me to continue, I will pursue you with clear intentions — not with pressure, but with respect.

One day, when distance is no longer between us, I hope we can look back at this beginning and smile at how something beautiful started with sincerity.

— Mico
